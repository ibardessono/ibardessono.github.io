Who is Isabelle?

Isabelle started her journey in to the world of teas as a senior in high school. Her senior project was about exploring tea, and her last semester was devoted completely to her project. During her project she interned at a tea company, and traveled to London to study afternoon teas. Her project was finished by a 50 minute presentation to her high school.

Since then she's discovered a love of tea, and has continued explore and learn more about tea. In the summer of 2016 she was given the opportunity to teach a class about tea for the Yountville Parks and Recreation department. Since then she's continued teaching classes, and has set out to share her passion for tea with others.
